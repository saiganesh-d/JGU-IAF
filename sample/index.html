About The Programme

The Master of Arts in Educational Leadership & Governance (MA-ELG), offered by the International Institute for Higher Education Research & Capacity Building (IIHEd) at O.P. Jindal Global University (JGU), is a one-year online postgraduate programme designed to develop transformative leaders who can drive institutional excellence, shape education policy, and lead systemic reform across schools, universities, and public education systems.

JGU is ranked #1 globally for Online Learning (THE, 2024 – Gold Category), making this a globally credible qualification for educators and education professionals. The programme equips participants with leadership frameworks, governance competencies, and strategic acumen needed to lead with vision, integrity, and lasting impact.
